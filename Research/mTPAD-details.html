Mobile Tethered Pelvic Assist Device (mTPAD) Motor Positions Optimization

Lab: Robotics And Rehabilitation (RoAR) Lab

This project focuses on optimizing motor positions for the Mobile Tethered Pelvic Assist Device (mTPAD) to improve performance in assisting pelvic movements.

Project Overview

In this project, we utilize advanced control algorithms to optimize the motor positions for enhanced assistance. This research helps in developing better robotic assistance for rehabilitation purposes.
