What is mTPAD - mobile tethered pelvic assist device

mTPAD is a cable-driven robotic platform. It can apply controllable forces to a lightweight pelvic belt.

mTPAD Control System

8 Motors Optimization Problem

Motivation

Previous mTPAD had 7 motors. It couldn't provide torque around the vertical axis. And the desired force and moment that can be reached are relatively small. Besides, there were some unsolved cases that lead to discontinuous wrench profile. So we plan to add the 8th momtor to overcome these problems.
