What is mTPAD - mobile tethered pelvic assist device

mTPAD is a cable-driven robotic platform. It can apply controllable forces to a lightweight pelvic belt.

mTPAD Control System

8 Motors Optimization Problem

Motivation

The previous mTPAD design, with 7 motors, could not provide torque around the vertical axis and had limited capacity to achieve desired force and moment outputs. Additionally, unresolved issues led to a discontinuous wrench profile. To address these limitations, we planned to add an 8th motor, enhancing performance and enabling smoother, more continuous force application.
