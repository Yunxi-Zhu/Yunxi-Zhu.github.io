Evolving Soft Robot

MECS4510 Evolutionary Computation and Design Automation, Fall 2023

In this project, I developed a physics-based simulation framework for spring-mass systems, enabling dynamic modeling of 3D structures. Implemented numerical methods to compute forces, accelerations, and trajectories of masses under the influence of springs, gravity, and collisions. Designed and visualized a dynamic spring-mass cube, optimizing parameters for stability and realistic motion. Extended the framework with advanced features like damping, friction, and external actuation to simulate complex behaviors.
